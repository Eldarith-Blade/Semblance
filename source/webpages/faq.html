FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Any questions related to technical equipment or in-game queries are located within this section

What is this game about?

Semblance is a completely immersive experience using the innovative technology of emotive adaption developed by Synaptic Studio specifically for this multimedia purpose. We, as a team, created this game for the purpose a testing an emerging technology that combines artifical intelligence with adaptive psychological probing. You play as a young man known as Eldarith set in the world of Auditori; the world itself is set within an amalgamation of cyberpunk and fantasy motifs. Explore the world freely interacting with the denizens of Auditori - however, be careful as your emotional state of being may affect your arching story that has a multitude of scenarios that may intertwine or seperate completely from the main objective.

How do I buy this game?

You have a variety of options to purchase this game. We are currently within early-access stage of development, so your current option is our Steam Store page. We currently have a timeline set for the complete release of the game and it will be available on many more platforms such as HumbleBundle, Xbox, PS4, and Steam. The game currently costs £15, but as development progresses and we near the final stage of development, the full release will be priced approximately at £35.

What do I need to play this game?

The A.E.P (Artifical Emotive Parser) is an in-house piece of software developed by our team to further your immersive experience within the game. The system is currently able to run efficiently and effectively on most webcams that operate with full-HD (1080p). There is an option within the settings to disable A.E.P, however, this may affect your story and immersive experience; you will still be able to play the game fully, but you will have less options than someone with A.E.P enabled and there will be less of a unique experience.
